The Problem

Paper Cards, Spreadsheets, and Costly Errors

  • Manual tallying errors invalidate results and create disputes on competition day
  • Spreadsheets have no institutional memory — shooter history is fragmented or lost entirely
  • Leaderboards and post-event reports take hours to compile by hand after every shoot
  • Generic software doesn't understand ATA/NSSA classes, event formats, or scoring rules
The Solution

Skeet.cc — One Dedicated Web App for Everything at the Range

A purpose-built Windows application that replaces paper and spreadsheets with a fast, accurate, offline-capable platform that speaks the language of the sport.
